About
Divorce Mate

Marriage issues go beyond legal procedures. They are emotional, personal, and impact many areas of life.

Divorce Mate offers structured guidance on:

  • ✦Understanding your relationship issues and whether counselling might help
  • ✦When separation should be considered
  • ✦What mutual separation looks like
  • ✦General court processes, without advertisements or promises

The emphasis is on direction, not pressure to pursue litigation.

Is divorce
right for me?

Deciding to end your marriage is a significant choice, and it's important to consider all your options carefully.

Things to Consider

Emotional readiness

Consider how you feel about ending the marriage and whether you're prepared for the emotional aspects of divorce.

Financial implications

Think about how divorce would impact your financial situation, including assets, debts, and alimony.

Co-parenting

If you have children, consider how you will co-parent and what arrangements would be best for both of you and the children.
